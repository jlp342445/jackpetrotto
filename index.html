About Jack

A Voice for the Standards

“You Will Be My Music”

A Buffalo native, Jack Petrotto has become a regional favorite by restyling songs from our Great American Songbook — elevating these classics with refined, thoughtful phrasing. From his first performances at local coffee shops and private events, Jack has been breathing new life into treasures like “The Shadow of Your Smile,” “Body and Soul,” and “Imagination,” setting a higher standard with every year.

“I've Got the World on a String”

While the Songbook is home, Jack connects just as personally with contemporary material, delivering every lyric with genuine commitment. His versatility surprises audiences in the best way — you might hear “Blue Moon” arranged as a country tune rather than the familiar 1934 Rodgers & Hart version, and love it all the more for it.

“The Best Is Yet to Come”

Jack's family traces its roots to Campobello, Sicily — and his voice carries that heritage proudly. His interpretive gifts add one more Italian-American singer to a long, celebrated tradition of excellence in music and art.

“It's a known fact that Italian Americans have made good pop singers… Italians have it in their genes to excel in music and art.”
— Tony Bennett
